About Me

Hello, my name is Peter Greaney.
Living in Galway, Barna, I make my living by being the Galway Handyman. In this job I get called up by clients looking to get a problem fixed or something new installed inside their home. I’ve been in the trades business’ for just over 30+ years. Starting off as a Stone Mason and later on getting into the Handyman side of things. The Stone Masonry thought me a lot of hard labour and experience that has prepared me for numerous jobs that I have completed. I’ve began the move into being a Handyman for smaller jobs as Stone Masonry can be very time consuming and physically draining. Those hardships prepared me for the Handyman jobs and I’m ready to do more if you want to give me a call!
